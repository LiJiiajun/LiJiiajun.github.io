About Me

I am an undergraduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at ZJU-UIUC Institute, Zhejiang University. In 2025, I participated in an exchange program at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Since March 2026, I have been working with Prof. Yang Yang's project group on Neuro-Knowledge Factory, an agent-oriented literature knowledge factory. My current interests include large language models, multimodal agents, embodied intelligence, EEG/BCI-oriented AI systems, and knowledge organization methods that support reasoning and action.

Research Interests

My recent work and reading focus on agent-oriented AI systems and neuro-AI applications, especially how models perceive information, structure knowledge, plan tasks, and support reasoning over neuroscience and BCI literature.

  • Multimodal and Embodied Agents: agents that use visual or environmental information for task planning, reasoning, and action decisions.
  • LLM-based Knowledge Systems: pipelines that turn unstructured papers into research cases, contribution profiles, working patterns, and agent-facing knowledge packages.
  • Neuro-AI and EEG/BCI: knowledge organization and agent-facing context construction for neuroscience, EEG, and brain-computer interface research.
